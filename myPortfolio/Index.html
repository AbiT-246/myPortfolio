Hi, I'm Abi

I’m a current computer science major and an aspiring software developer based in Calgary, Alberta.

Before getting into the nitty-gritty, scroll down and check out some of the fun stuff I like to do…
Oh, and make sure to give my rainy day playlist above a listen as you scroll!

01
Poetry

Writing has always been my way of expressing myself and making sense of the world around me. Through that journey, I came to learn that fewer words make the greatest impact. My poetry has touched the lives of many people through my instagram presence, and even went on to win international competitions, showing the world what a girl with a pen can do.

02
Photography

I don’t believe there is a greater gift than being able to capture and preserve a beautiful moment in time and relive it through a piece of paper (or screen); that belief is what drew me to photography. Immersed in various communities, cultures, and breathtaking landscapes, I have been able to bring this gift to fruition many times, and sharing the magic of my experiences with others brings me immense joy.

03
Supporting Women in Tech

Coming from a society where a person's background often hinders their potential regardless of their talent, being able to help women and minorities on campus overcome similar obstacles has played a huge role in sculpting my identity. Being a part of the Women in Computer Science Club at school and advocating for technology as an essential tool that everyone should have the right to leverage has been one of my favorite experiences. Along the way, I've also had the privilege of meeting incredible individuals and forming lasting friendships.
