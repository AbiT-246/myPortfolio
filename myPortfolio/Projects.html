WeatherMate

This was one of my first dips into the world of front-end web development and APIs. WeatherMate will give you the current weather for almost every city worldwide, along with extra wind, pressure, and temperature data.

VerseHunt

VerseHunt is a poetry search-engine that opens the door to a treasure trove of beautiful words penned by countless gifted writers and lyricists who have graced this world. With the poetryDB API at its core, it grants users access to an extensive collection of timeless classic poems. To ensure inclusivity, VerseHunt offers a read-aloud feature, allowing poetry to be enjoyed by all. And that's not all! Soon, it will also boast a translation feature, further enriching the poetic experience.

Peek

Peek is a website that tackles one of the most pressing concerns of our modern world: lazy people trying to create new passwords. By harnessing the power of diverse APIs and algorithms, Peek assesses the relative strength of a user-provided password against multiple factors. But that's not all! Peek goes the extra mile by generating a new password for users, cleverly incorporating fragments of their personal information. This ingenious approach not only enhances security but also guarantees better memorability. Say goodbye to password woes with Peek!
